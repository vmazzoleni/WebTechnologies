My Blog Post

Tomato soup is one of the great comfort foods of the world. With just a handful of ingredients, it transforms humble tomatoes into something silky, rich, and deeply satisfying.

It warms you up on cold days, pairs beautifully with a grilled cheese sandwich, and is ready in under 30 minutes. What more could you ask for?

The secret is to roast the tomatoes first — it concentrates their sweetness and adds a subtle smokiness that makes all the difference.
