Chicken Feet Soup

Chicken feet soup is a traditional dish that is popular in many countries. The feet release collagen while cooking, creating a rich and flavorful broth. This recipe is simple and perfect for cold days.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken feet
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 litre stock
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Wash the chicken feet carefully.
  2. Chop the vegetables.
  3. Heat a large pot and add the ingredients.
  4. Pour in the stock.
  5. Cook for about 2 hours.
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot.

Tip: The longer the soup cooks, the richer the broth becomes.
